Who am I?

I'm Kjani Van Eenaeme, a cybersecurity student at Howest focused on offensive security and pentesting. I'm currently building a honeypot webstore application from scratch — Python backend, React SPA frontend — and I do CTF challenges on TryHackMe in my spare time.

What do I do?

I'm hands-on by nature. I run my own homelab with Proxmox and OPNsense, self-host my own services, and enjoy understanding how systems break just as much as how they're built. I also co-founded and help manage the Howest Campus Stack Discord community — a 650+ member platform for IT students at Howest.

What's my goal?

To grow into offensive security consulting and eventually run my own boutique pentesting firm in Belgium. I'm open to cybersecurity-related student opportunities in Belgium.
